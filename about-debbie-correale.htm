Meet Debbie Correale Owner and Founder of Redesign Right, Interior Decorator, Redesigner and Certified Color, Redesign and Staging Instructor

Debbie Correale
"I love the challenge of working with what you own and helping you to fall in love with your home all over again!"

Debbie is a ten-time award winning interior redesigner, home stager, and trainer. With over 12 years in the business, Debbie is sought after by homeowners and realtors for interior redesign, home staging, color trends and as a training provider for those seeking to get into the redesign and staging business.

Debbie has been rearranging rooms since she was a young girl, and it was this incredible passion for people and interior redesign and staging that led her to start Redesign Right, LLC. She has lived in every region of the USA and has worked with all home types and styles. She has compassion and knows what it is like to buy, sell and set up houses as she has had to experience it herself over a dozen times!

Oliver Correale- Vice President

Debbie is the founder and owner of The Foundations for Redesigning and Staging Right training program (FRSR). The FRSR Certified 5-Day Interior Redesign and Home Staging hands-on training program is offered in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area. This program is designed for students seeking to turn their passion for design into a fun and rewarding career.

"I love to help entrepreneurs achieve their dreams of owning their own interior redesign and home staging business."

Redesign Right also provides an FRSR Certified 3-Day Interior Redesign Training program for those who love to design and want to learn more about decorating but are unsure if they want to make it a business.

In her previous career, Debbie was a buyer of home furnishings and accessories for a national multi-chain department store in Boston. Her job gave her the opportunity to travel and expand her design knowledge. During the years she was raising her four children; she ran a home-based business providing homeowners with decorating solutions using furniture and accessories.

Redesign Right is currently serving the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area servicing Chester, Delaware, Montgomery, Philadelphia, Lancaster and Lehigh counties, also servicing select areas in Delaware and New Jersey.
