Meet Debbie Correale Owner and Founder of Redesign Right, Home Stager, Interior Decorator, Redesigner, Certified Color Consultant

Debbie Correale - Owner, Founder, Certified Trainer
"I love working with what you own and helping you to fall in love with your home all over again!""

Since I was a young girl, I have been rearranging rooms, and it was this incredible passion for people and home design, decorating, and staging that led me to start Redesign Right, LLC. I have lived in every region of the USA and worked with all home types and styles. As someone who has bought, sold, and moved 15 times, I understand the process and know the stress involved. I have over 20 years of experience as a professional redesigner and home stager running a successful business.

I was a buyer of home furnishings and accessories for a national multi-chain department store in Boston in my previous career. During those years, my job allowed me to travel and expand my design knowledge. I was also raising our four children; I ran a home-based business providing homeowners with decorating solutions using furniture and accessories.

I have won multiple awards in interior redesign, home staging and have received Best of Houzz over numerous years. I work with homeowners and realtors for interior redesign, home staging, and color trends.

Oliver Correale- Vice President

Debbie is the founder and owner of The Foundations for Redesigning and Staging Right training program (FRSR). The FRSR Certified 5-Day Interior Redesign and Home Staging hands-on training program is offered in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area. This program is designed for students seeking to turn their passion for design into a fun and rewarding career.

"I love to help entrepreneurs achieve their dreams of owning their own interior redesign and home staging business."

Redesign Right also provides an FRSR Certified 3-Day Interior Redesign Training program for those who love to design and want to learn more about decorating but are unsure if they're going to make it a business.
