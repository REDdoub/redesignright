3-Day Certified Interior Redesign Training Program

The Certified 3-day Interior Redesign Training program is a fun, interactive class designed for the student who loves decorating and wants to learn more about design and color selection yet does not wish to start a business. Students will learn and observe through classroom and hands-on training and will earn an FRSR Interior Redesign Certification.

to learn more about this training program, view our upcoming session dates and to sign up.

Why sign up for our training program?

  • Debbie has been certified by three different design and staging associations to be their trainer.
  • With over 12 years of running a successful redesign and home staging business, she brings plenty of business knowledge, life experiences and fun energy to the training classroom.
  • The FRSR Interior Redesign 3-day program teaches you the basics of redesign and will help you understand the techniques of furniture arranging, proper color selection and balanced accessorizing.
  • This program only allows a maximum of six students. Classes held in the suburbs of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
