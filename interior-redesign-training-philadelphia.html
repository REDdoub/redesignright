3-Day Certified Interior Redesign Training Program

The Certified 3-day Interior Redesign Training program is a fun, interactive class designed for the student who loves decorating and wants to learn more about design and color selection yet does not wish to start a business. Students will learn and observe through classroom and hands-on training and will earn an FRSR Interior Redesign Certification.

Why sign up for our training program?

  • Debbie has been trained, certified and recognized by three industry trade associations to teach Color, Interior Redesign and Real Estate Home Staging training.
