Our Stress-free process:

  1. We start with a one-to-one consultation to establish your lifestyle, tastes, hopes and dreams.
  2. Together, we develop a "Design Plan" that evaluates paint colors, what you already own and what may need to be purchased to complete the design.
  3. We discuss the priorities of the project, time frame, and your budget; no job is too big or too small (single room or entire home).
  4. From the plan, you may elect to do all the work yourself, or choose Redesign Right's stress-free full-service package that will complete the look in one day, just like you see on TV!

Redesign Right specializes in giving stale rooms strong foundations that flow and final touches that create pop and excitement.
