5-Day Certified Interior Redesign & Home Staging Training Program

Do you have a passion for home decorating and want to start your own business?

Redesign Right offers a Certified 5-Day Interior Redesign and Home Staging Training class that is intense, exciting and prepares students with the foundation to start a successful redesign and home staging business.

  • The program aims to educate, prepare and inspire confidence in students to start their own successful decorating business.
  • Students will learn and observe through classroom and hands-on experience. They will actively use their new skills as they physically decorate and redesign in three real clients' homes.
  • Classes are Monday-Friday or split over two weekends in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania area.
  • Upon completion of the 5-day program, students will earn two certified designations: FRSR Certified Interior Redesigner and FRSR Certified Real Estate Home Stager.
